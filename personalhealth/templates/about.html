About Us Page

The Personal Health Record Management system helps to maintain the health records.

It's a user friendly system. You can upload and maintain all the health records like scan reports, blood test reports etc.

This System will be very helpful for all the users when they fall sick unexpectedly and want to meet a doctor immediately.

Here you can even upload the prescription or add the name of the tablet which the doctor had prescribed and even note when the tablet should be taken.
